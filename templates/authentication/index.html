What is Moviesyn?

Moviesyn is a movie recommendation engine that utilizes collaborative filtering. We plan to open-source the engine later in development.

Movies tailored to your taste.

Moviesyn helps you find movies that could be your next favorites. Build a profile based on your current favorites, and let Moviesyn do the rest.

When can I get access?

While the project is under development, you can join the closed beta by signing up. We will email you for access to the project. Moviesyn is planned to be completed in May of 2023.
